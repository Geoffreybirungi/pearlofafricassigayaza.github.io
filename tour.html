Did you know that?

Pearl of Africa Secondary School is a mixed day and boarding school. The school population has been growing steadily over the years having started as a single stream school to the current status with a student population of 800 learners. In academic performance the school is one of the best public mixed day schools in Igayaza Town. In 2022 UNEB Exam, 12 of the candidates were admitted to the university. The school infrastructure has continued to improve. Modern facilities have been put in place to cater for the growing population. Currently the school has 22 Classrooms . There are 3 Science laboratories each having a capacity of 60 students as well as a Home Science lab with a capacity of 45 students. There is need for a bigger and more appropriate Home Science room. There is a Computer laboratory with a Capacity of 40 Students for computer studies. The school has 1 library, 1 Geography room, 1 Kitchen (Semi permanent). There are 3 water sources – Rain water, Uganda National piped water and water bowsers. There are 10 boys’ toilets with one Special need toilet, which serves the boys at a ratio 30:1 . Girls Toilets are 13, at a ratio of 25:1.

Pearl of Africa Secondary school

Pearl of Africa Secondary school is a Mixed day School both 'A' and 'O' level.

The school is located in Igayaza town board (along Hoima-Kakumiro), Birembo sub-county in Bugangaizi west constituency in Kakumiro-Kibaale Districts . The school offers the traditional curriculum as per the Uganda national Examination Board. In addition to this, it has a unique program that provides practical skills and value based lessons that holistically prepare the learner for the changing world.
