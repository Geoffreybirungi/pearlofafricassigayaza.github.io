About Us

MORE LITERATURE ABOUT THE SCHOOLL

Pearl of Africa secondary school is a value based secondary school located in Igayaza town board (along Hoima-Kakumiro), Birembo sub-county in Bugangaizi west constituency in Kakumiro District. The school concentrates on providing quality subsidized education to the community around in order to create for themselves the life they want. The founders are trained, knowledgeable, experienced and professionals by calling, who are responsible and passionate educationist to offer a unique education that pays for its self. /p>

Status: Pearl secondary school is private mixed, ”O” and “A” level, Day and boarding, offering both Arts and sciences at all levels. It offers the traditional curriculum as per the Uganda national Examination Board. In addition to this, it has a unique program that provides practical skills and value based lessons that holistically prepare the learner for the changing world.
